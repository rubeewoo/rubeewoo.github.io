Structural Analysis

Thesis Statement

In the opening paragraph [Sec. #1], the first sentence as highlighted in the following quotes would be the thesis statement. Firstly it would be because of the biodiversity loss in the sentence, which is the central idea of this article and it is also a part of the article title.

Corporate Australia is familiar with the concept that climate change presents a financial risk to the global economy, but more recently biodiversity loss has emerged as an equally important risk. In fact, climate change and biodiversity loss are now often referred to as the “twin crises” facing the global financial system and awareness of the role the financial sector plays in this is rising swiftly.

Essay Outline

  • [Sec. #2] - Connection Between Nature Risks and Financial System

    The topic of this section is made very clear with the phrase economic system is dependent on biodiversity and potential parallels between nature risk and other responsibilities of financial institutionsin this section. The section talks about how biodiversity loss along with other natural crisis can affect the economic and financial system.

  • [Sec. #3] - Work Being Done on Trying to End Nature Risks

    In the first sentence of this section, the main phrase is an international Taskforce on Nature-related Financial Disclosures (TNFD) launched last month. That phrase is telling the readers that the officials are taking actions. In fact, the whole first paragraph of section 3 is talking about what work is going to be done.

  • [Sec. #4] - Nature Is a Part of Company Directors' Duties

    This section is talking about how the nature related physical risks are connected to companies. The phrase a director who fails to properly consider these risks could be held personally liable for breaching their “duty of due care and diligence” to the company talks how their actions towards the nature can affect their company's interests.

  • [Sec. #5] - Ditching Nautre-Negative for Nature-Positive

    This section is about how investors and consumers might be more interested in eco-friendly products. From Consumer preference for sustainability conscious products has been growing for some time. and has demonstrated that there is emerging appetite for investment in nature-positive assets at scale. it is clear that people are more towards being friendly to the nature.

[Sec. #2]

Coherence

  • Para #1 - The first paragraph of Section #2 is the paragraph following the thesis paragraph, so it is started with Crucially, to continue on the topic of biodiversity loss and the importance of it. This paragraph also talks about how economic system is dependent on biodiversity, which makes it clear that it is one of the subtopic of this article.
  • Para #2 - The second paragraph of this section continues on the topic of how fincancial and economics are connected to the nature and biodiversity. The phrase potential parallels between nature risk and other responsibilities of financial institutions made it clear that the author is making connections between economics and biodiversity.

Cohesion

  • Para #1 - In the second sentence of the first paragraph, biodiversity is declining, the author is saying that biodiveristy is disappearing. The author then uses biodiversity loss in the second paragraph to paraphrase the phrase in the first paragraph.
