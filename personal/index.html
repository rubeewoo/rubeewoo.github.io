Autobiography

Hello everyone! My name is Ruby Wu and this is my website. I'm currently a FLL freshmen in NTHU. I used to live in Thailand before I came to study here so I'm not used to many things in Taiwan yet. I really enjoy playing volleyball, so that's why I joined FLL's girls volleyball team.
